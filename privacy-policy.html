Privacy Policy

How Arvani Media collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: February 26, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

Arvani Media ("Arvani Media," "we," "our," or "us") is a done-for-you B2B outbound agency headquartered in the United States. We operate the website arvani-media.com and provide cold email marketing, LinkedIn outreach, CRM setup, and AI-powered outbound services to B2B companies.

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: anthony@arvani-media.com or call 508-815-4211.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you fill out a form on our website — including our "Claim 25 Free Leads" form or strategy call booking form — we collect:

  • Name and business name
  • Business email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Company URL and industry (if provided)
  • Any additional information you include in message fields

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we and our service providers may automatically collect:

  • IP address and general geographic location (country/region)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URL
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 5)

B2B Contact Data Used in Client Services

As part of delivering our outbound services, we source and process business contact data (name, business email, job title, company name) on behalf of our clients. This data is sourced from professional data providers and represents publicly available business information. We use this data solely to carry out the services we have been engaged to provide. We do not sell this data or use it for purposes outside the agreed client engagement.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To fulfill service requests — deliver free lead samples, schedule strategy calls, and provide our outbound services
  • To communicate with you — respond to inquiries, send appointment confirmations, and follow up on service discussions
  • To send marketing communications — with your consent or where permitted by law, send emails about our services, case studies, and outbound tips. You can opt out at any time.
  • To improve our website and services — analyze how visitors use our site and optimize content and user experience
  • To operate AI-assisted tools — we use automated systems (including AI classification) to process email replies and surface relevant leads on behalf of clients. These systems categorize responses but do not make binding decisions about individuals without human review.
  • To comply with legal obligations — maintain records as required by applicable law

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent — when you submit a form or opt in to marketing communications
  • Legitimate interests — to follow up on genuine business inquiries, conduct B2B outreach on behalf of clients, and improve our services. We have assessed that our legitimate interests do not override the rights and freedoms of data subjects in the context of professional B2B communication.
  • Contractual necessity — to deliver services you have engaged us to perform
  • Legal compliance — to meet applicable regulatory requirements

For cross-border data transfers from the EEA to the United States, we rely on the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (where applicable) or Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as the legal mechanism for transfer.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to operate correctly and understand how visitors use the site. These include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (e.g., session management, form functionality)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand traffic patterns and page performance
  • Third-party cookies — may be set by embedded tools such as our scheduling widget or chat widget

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. You may also opt out of interest-based advertising through industry opt-out tools such as the NAI opt-out tool or the DAA's opt-out program. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Third-Party Services and Data Processors

We use the following third-party services that may process your data on our behalf:

  • LeadConnector / GoHighLevel — our CRM, form processing, and scheduling platform. All form submissions and booked calls are stored in our LeadConnector account. See GoHighLevel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — loads web fonts from Google's CDN. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — our website hosting provider, which processes server logs including visitor IP addresses. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not share it with parties outside the services listed above except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

7. Your Privacy Rights by Jurisdiction

All Users

Regardless of your location, you may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. We will respond within 30 days.

California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know — request disclosure of personal information we have collected, used, disclosed, or sold about you
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Correct — request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing — we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information — we do not collect sensitive personal information (as defined by CPRA) from website visitors
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights

To submit a California privacy request, email us at anthony@arvani-media.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request."

Other U.S. State Privacy Laws

Residents of Colorado (CPA), Virginia (VCDPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Texas (TDPSA), Oregon, Montana, and other states with applicable privacy laws have similar rights to access, correct, delete, and opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal data. Please contact us to exercise these rights. Note that many state privacy laws exempt data processed in a strictly business-to-business (B2B) context.

EEA / UK Residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have additional rights including: the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, the right to object to automated decision-making, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (DPA).

8. Automated Decision-Making and AI

We use AI-assisted tools as part of our service delivery — primarily for classifying cold email replies (e.g., interested, not interested, out of office) and enriching lead data. These tools assist human decision-making but do not make binding decisions about individuals. No automated system makes final determinations that significantly affect your rights or opportunities without a human review step.

9. CAN-SPAM and CASL Compliance

All commercial emails sent by or on behalf of Arvani Media comply with the CAN-SPAM Act and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) where applicable:

  • We clearly identify ourselves as the sender in all commercial emails
  • We include a valid physical mailing address in all commercial emails
  • We honor opt-out requests promptly — within 10 business days for CAN-SPAM and 10 business days for CASL
  • We do not use deceptive subject lines or misleading header information
  • We maintain suppression lists to prevent re-emailing opted-out contacts

To opt out of marketing emails, click the unsubscribe link in any email or contact us at anthony@arvani-media.com.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal data according to the following schedule:

  • Form submissions and leads — retained for the duration of the business relationship plus 3 years for record-keeping
  • Client service data — retained for the duration of the engagement plus 5 years to satisfy applicable contractual and regulatory requirements
  • Website analytics data — retained for up to 26 months in an aggregated or anonymized form
  • Financial and billing records — retained for 7 years in accordance with U.S. tax law requirements

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time. We will comply within 30 days except where retention is required by law.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended exclusively for business professionals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has submitted information through our site, we will delete it promptly upon notice.

12. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data, including access controls, encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS), and regular security reviews of our service providers. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, service offerings, or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also notify affected users by email. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or data rights requests related to this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: anthony@arvani-media.com
  • Phone: 508-815-4211
  • Website: arvani-media.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 30 days. If you are an EEA or UK resident and we are unable to resolve your request, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
