Buying Signal Server

Most outbound targets cold prospects at random. Our signal server flips that model — monitoring the web daily to identify companies and contacts that are showing real buying signals right now, before your competitors even know they're in market.

We build a custom signal stack around your ICP: the specific triggers that indicate a company is likely to buy what you sell. When those signals fire, those contacts get prioritized in your campaigns immediately.

  • Website visitor identification — see exactly which companies are visiting your site and reach out while they're actively researching
  • 1,250 website visitor leads identified per month (upgradeable based on traffic volume)
  • Contact enrichment for every site visitor — company, title, email, LinkedIn
  • Dedicated email + LinkedIn campaigns built specifically for visitor outreach
  • Job change, funding, tech stack, and engagement signal monitoring
  • Daily signal processing so high-intent leads get surfaced in real time

Qualification, Enrichment & Scoring Bot

Every lead we generate runs through a multi-layer AI pipeline before a single email goes out. The bot enriches each contact with company and personal data, qualifies them against your ICP criteria, then scores them — so your campaigns focus firepower on the leads most likely to convert.

Rather than treating a 3,000-contact list as equal, our scoring system creates a hierarchy. The highest-scoring contacts get prioritized in campaign order, in follow-up frequency, and in LinkedIn targeting. Low-quality leads are deprioritized or filtered out entirely.

  • Automated enrichment: company size, revenue, tech stack, funding stage, LinkedIn activity
  • ICP qualification scoring against your defined criteria
  • Composite lead score used to rank every contact before campaigns launch
  • Continuous rescoring as new signals come in — scores update in real time
  • Seamless handoff to email and LinkedIn campaigns with scoring data attached

Pre-Meeting Research Bot

The moment a meeting gets booked, our research bot goes to work. It pulls together everything your sales rep needs to know about the company and the contact — without them having to spend an hour digging through LinkedIn and Google before the call.

A structured briefing document is automatically generated and sent to your rep before every meeting — so every call starts with full context, not blind introductions.

  • Company overview: what they do, size, market, key products, recent news
  • Contact profile: role, tenure, background, LinkedIn activity, recent posts
  • Likely pain points mapped to your solution based on company profile
  • Potential objections flagged with suggested responses
  • Conversation starters and recommended talking points
  • Delivered as a clean PDF to your sales rep's inbox before the meeting
