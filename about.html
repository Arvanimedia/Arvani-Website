Meet The Founder

Photo Coming Soon

Anthony Volz

Founder & CEO, Arvani Media

Anthony studied at Bryant University, where a marketing class introduced him to CRMs and sales automation. That was it — he spent the next few months going deep: reading, testing, building. By the time he understood how these systems actually worked, he had already decided to start something.

He launched Arvani Media and built Arvani Pro — a full CRM and automation platform — not because he planned to, but because the clients he was talking to needed it. He used cold email to find his first clients, and it worked. He started landing B2B accounts, implementing outbound systems at small scale, and learning exactly what moved the needle.

Entrepreneurship was always the goal. His father ran his own construction company before he passed — that example stuck. Anthony wanted to build something of his own, and he was willing to figure it out the hard way.

He expanded into B2C work too — building websites and CRM systems for businesses outside the traditional B2B world. Then things got harder. His mother's health declined, and he had to step back from growing the agency. He didn't stop working — he used that time to go deeper on email marketing and AI automation at scale. He studied what was working across industries, refined his systems, and built the infrastructure behind what Arvani Media delivers today.

The agency is built on what he learned the hard way — and it runs on systems he'd trust for his own growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who founded Arvani Media?+
Arvani Media was founded by Anthony Volz. He studied at Bryant University, where a marketing class sparked his interest in CRMs and automation. After months of research, he launched Arvani Media and built Arvani Pro CRM — using cold email to find his first clients and developing systems that solve the core outbound problems: bad data, poor deliverability, and no way to sort replies at scale.
Where is Arvani Media based?+
Arvani Media is based in the United States and serves B2B companies nationwide. All services are delivered remotely, so we work with clients across every time zone.
What makes Arvani Media different from other lead gen agencies?+
Three things: infrastructure, AI, and accountability. We build dedicated sending infrastructure for every client (not shared pools), use AI to classify every reply in real time, and structure engagements as 4-month commitments — because real outbound systems take time to build, warm, and optimize before they deliver consistent results.
