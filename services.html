Our 6-Step Process

From onboarding to booked meetings, here's exactly what happens when you work with Arvani Media.

1

Discovery & ICP Building

We start with a kickoff call to understand your business, offer, and ideal customer. We define target industries, company sizes, job titles, geographic focus, and exclusion criteria. This becomes the blueprint for everything that follows.

2

Lead Sourcing & Verification

We source ICP-matched contacts from multiple verified data providers, qualifying every record against your criteria. Each lead is verified for email deliverability, title accuracy, and company fit. Catch-all, invalid, and risky addresses are removed. You approve the list before a single email sends.

3

Infrastructure Setup

We register dedicated sending domains, create mailboxes, and configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication. A proper 4-week warmup runs with a slow ramp — warmup emails continue running even after campaigns go live. Mailbox health is monitored throughout, with a full rehab process if any mailbox degrades.

4

Campaign Writing & Launch

Our copywriters craft personalized email sequences tailored to your offer and audience. Every campaign includes A/B tests on subject lines, opening lines, and CTAs. Once warmup is complete, campaigns launch automatically with optimized sending schedules.

5

AI Reply Classification

As replies come in, our AI model reads each one and classifies it in real time — interested, not interested, out of office, wrong person, unsubscribe, referral, and more. Interested leads are flagged immediately so you can follow up while they're warm. Opt-out requests are processed automatically.

6

Booking & Optimization

Interested prospects are routed to your calendar. Your AI assistant or sales rep takes the conversation from there and closes. Meanwhile, we analyze performance data, optimize what's working, test new angles, and scale what delivers results.

Every reply to your campaigns is read and categorized by AI in real time. No manual sorting. No missed opportunities.

How It Works

When a prospect replies to your cold email, our AI model analyzes the message content, sentiment, and intent. It classifies the reply into one of several categories and takes automatic action based on the classification.

Interested replies are flagged immediately for your team or AI assistant to follow up — sharing a calendar link when the timing is right. Opt-out requests are processed automatically to maintain compliance. You get notified only when action is needed — saving hours of manual inbox sorting every week.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?+
Month 1 is fully dedicated to infrastructure: ICP definition, lead sourcing, domain registration, mailbox creation, DNS configuration, and a proper 4-week warmup ramp. Campaigns go live in month 2. Every plan is a 4-month commitment — after that, you can continue, upgrade, or move to maintenance mode.
What's included in lead verification?+
Every lead goes through multi-step verification: email deliverability check, catch-all detection, title validation against your ICP, company size confirmation, and removal of risky or invalid addresses. This keeps bounce rates under 2%.
What happens in Month 1?+
Month 1 is your setup and infrastructure month. We build your lead lists, configure your sending domains, write your sequences, and warm up your mailboxes. No live outbound runs during Month 1 — everything is built for a clean launch in Month 2.
What is SPF, DKIM, and DMARC?+
These are email authentication protocols. SPF tells email providers which servers can send on your behalf. DKIM adds a digital signature to verify the email wasn't altered. DMARC tells providers what to do with emails that fail SPF or DKIM checks. Together, they prove your emails are legitimate and improve deliverability.
How does AI reply classification work?+
Our AI model reads every reply to your campaigns and categorizes them into predefined buckets: interested, not interested, out of office, wrong person, unsubscribe, referral, and more. Interested leads are flagged immediately for fast follow-up. The system handles unsubscribe requests automatically to maintain compliance.
Can I target multiple ICPs or offers?+
Yes. The Run plan is designed for multi-ICP and multi-offer targeting. You can run separate campaigns for different buyer personas, product lines, or service offerings simultaneously with dedicated sequences for each.
What metrics can I track?+
You get full visibility into deliverability rates, open rates, reply rates, positive vs negative sentiment, meetings booked, and campaign-level A/B test results. Walk and Run plans include regular reports; Run includes a real-time dashboard.
Do you handle compliance and opt-outs?+
Absolutely. Every campaign includes unsubscribe handling, suppression list management, and CAN-SPAM compliance. AI classification automatically flags and processes opt-out requests. We also maintain do-not-contact lists across all campaigns.
