Why Multi-Channel Outreach Outperforms Email Alone

Most prospects won't respond to a single channel. Decision-makers are flooded with email — but a coordinated sequence that reaches them on LinkedIn too signals legitimacy, builds familiarity, and dramatically increases the chance they reply.

Our LinkedIn campaigns aren't run separately from email. They're built around the same ICP, the same contacts, and the same campaign goals — creating a unified outreach experience that compounds reply rates across both channels.

The result: the same prospect gets a thoughtful email sequence and a LinkedIn connection or message sequence. Recognizing your name on two platforms makes you a warm contact, not a cold one.
