How It Works

A six-step process that starts conversations and turns interested prospects into booked meetings — fully managed by our team from day one.

1

ICP & Offer Development

We work with you to define your ideal customer profile — target industries, company sizes, titles, geographies, and exclusion criteria — then pressure-test your offer and messaging angle so every email resonates with the right buyer.

2

Lead Sourcing & Verification

Contacts sourced from verified data providers and AI-qualified against your ICP. Every address is verified for deliverability — catch-all and risky addresses removed — so your list is clean before a single email goes out.

3

Infrastructure Setup

Dedicated sending domains registered with full SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication. Mailboxes created, connected, and warmed up over 4 weeks to build sender reputation before campaigns go live.

4

Campaign & Sequence Build

Personalized 3-touch sequences written by outbound specialists. Subject lines, opening hooks, value propositions, and CTAs — all crafted to sound human, not templated. Multiple offers and angles built for A/B testing from the start.

5

Launch & Ongoing Optimization

Campaigns go live with controlled sending volume, gradually scaling as sender reputation strengthens. We continuously monitor open rates, reply rates, and deliverability — adjusting subject lines, sequences, and targeting based on what performs.

6

Reply Management & Conversation Routing

Our AI Reply Classifier labels every reply in real time — interested, not interested, OOO, wrong person, referral. Interested replies are flagged immediately and your team (or our AI assistant) continues the conversation — sharing a calendar link when the timing is right. Opt-outs handled automatically.
