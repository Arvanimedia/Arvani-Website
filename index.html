Frequently Asked Questions

What is done-for-you cold email? +
Done-for-you cold email means Arvani Media handles every part of your outbound email system — from building verified lead lists and setting up dedicated sending domains to writing personalized campaigns and classifying replies with AI. You get booked meetings without lifting a finger.
How quickly can I expect results? +
Most clients see their first qualified replies within 2 weeks of campaign launch. Full ramp-up takes about 30 days as we warm your sending domains and optimize messaging based on real response data.
Will cold email hurt my domain reputation? +
No. We never send from your primary domain. We set up separate sending domains with proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication, then warm them gradually. Your main domain stays completely protected.
What industries do you work with? +
We work with any B2B company that sells to other businesses — SaaS, marketing agencies, consulting firms, financial services, IT services, commercial real estate, staffing, and more. If your ideal customer has a business email, cold email works.
How are leads verified? +
Every lead goes through multi-step verification: we confirm the email is deliverable, validate the contact still works at the company, check the title matches your ICP, and remove catch-all and risky addresses. This keeps bounce rates under 2%.
What does AI reply classification do? +
Our AI reads every reply to your campaigns and categorizes them in real time — interested, not interested, out of office, wrong person, do not contact, and more. Interested leads get flagged immediately so you can follow up while they're hot. Unsubscribe requests are handled automatically.
Do I need any tools or software? +
No. We provide everything: sending infrastructure, lead data, campaign software, and AI classification. You just need a calendar link so we can book meetings directly. Optionally, Arvani Pro (our CRM) can manage your full pipeline.
What is the commitment? +
Every plan requires a 4-month commitment. Month 1 is dedicated to building your infrastructure — domain setup, warmup, lead sourcing, and campaign launch. Months 2–4 run active campaigns. After month 4, you can choose to continue at the same rate, upgrade to a higher plan, or transition to a lower-cost maintenance mode to re-engage your built database quarterly.
